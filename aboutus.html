Our Founders

Find Your Home is a collaborative project brought to life by a dynamic quintet of aspiring developers: Faizan Khan, Yassir Farouqui, Amir Hamza Khan, Haider Ali Khan and Alizeh Sheikh. As students in a Database Management Systems (DBMS) course, we recognized the potential to combine our skills and knowledge to create a platform that simplifies the home-finding process.

Our website is not just a theoretical project; it is a practical application of our learning, seamlessly integrating SQL to fetch queries and deliver real-time data to users. With a shared passion for technology and a commitment to enhancing user experiences, we are dedicated to making Find Your Home a reliable resource for anyone seeking a place to call their own.
