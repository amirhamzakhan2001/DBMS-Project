1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information from users to provide and improve our Services, enable transactions, and ensure a secure experience. The types of information we collect include:

1.1 Personal Information

When you register an account, use our Services, or communicate with us, you may provide us with the following personal information:

  • Name: Your full name to create an account and personalize your experience.
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, and mailing address for communication and verification.
  • Login Credentials: Username and password to secure your account.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, bank account numbers, and transaction history for rental payments or property purchases.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

We also collect non-identifying information, such as:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and mobile carrier.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our Services, including the pages you visit, the time spent on our website or app, and features used.
  • Location Data: If you enable location services, we may collect geolocation information to provide location-based services (e.g., properties near you).

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior. Cookies are small files that are stored on your device and help us understand how you use our Services. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the functionality of our Services.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze website traffic and performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Allow us to remember your preferences and customize your experience.
  • Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver personalized ads based on your interests.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling certain cookies may limit your experience with our Services.

We use your information to:

We use aggregated and anonymized data to:

  • Analyze trends, monitor user behavior, and improve our Services.

We take data security seriously and implement various safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

Despite our efforts, no security measure is completely foolproof. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials and notifying us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.
